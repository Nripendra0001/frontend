🔍 Explore Major Government Exams

India ke top government exam categories – ek jagah sab kuch.

👮 UP Police
  • Eligibility: 12th pass
  • Age Limit: 18–25 years
  • Salary: ₹21,700 – ₹69,100 (Level-3)
  • Selection: Written → PET → PST → Medical
  • Physical: Mandatory (running, height, chest)
  • Career Path: Constable → SI → Inspector → DSP
  • Posting: Uttar Pradesh
  • Facilities: Pension, uniform, risk allowance
🏢 SSC Exams
  • Major Exams: CGL, CHSL, GD, MTS, Steno
  • Eligibility: 10th / 12th / Graduate
  • Age Limit: 18–32 years
  • Salary: ₹18,000 – ₹1,42,000
  • Selection: Tier-1 → Tier-2 → Skill/Descriptive
  • Jobs: Income Tax, CBI, Auditor, Clerk
  • Posting: All India
  • Facilities: DA, HRA, promotions
🚆 Railway
  • Major Exams: NTPC, Group D, ALP, JE
  • Eligibility: 10th / ITI / Diploma / Graduate
  • Age Limit: 18–33 years
  • Salary: ₹18,000 – ₹1,12,000
  • Selection: CBT → CBT-2 → DV → Medical
  • Jobs: Station Master, Clerk, Technician
  • Posting: Zonal railways
  • Facilities: Free pass, quarters, medical
🏦 Banking
  • Major Exams: IBPS PO/Clerk, SBI PO/Clerk, RRB
  • Eligibility: Graduate
  • Age Limit: 20–30 years
  • Salary: ₹23,000 – ₹85,000+
  • Selection: Prelims → Mains → Interview
  • Jobs: Clerk, PO, Manager
  • Posting: Banks across India
  • Facilities: Medical, loans, fast promotion
📚 Teaching
  • Major Exams: CTET, UPTET, Super TET, DSSSB
  • Eligibility: D.El.Ed / B.Ed + TET
  • Age Limit: 21–40 years
  • Salary: ₹35,000 – ₹1,12,000
  • Selection: Written → Merit → DV
  • Jobs: Primary, TGT, PGT
  • Posting: Schools
  • Facilities: Long leaves, stable life
🪖 Defence
  • Major Exams: NDA, CDS, Agniveer, AFCAT
  • Eligibility: 10th / 12th / Graduate
  • Age Limit: 16.5 – 27 years
  • Salary: ₹30,000 – ₹2,00,000+
  • Selection: Written → SSB → Medical
  • Life: Discipline, adventure, respect
  • Posting: All India
  • Facilities: Canteen, pension, housing

📁 Government Exam Document & Mistake Hub

Sabhi government exams ke documents, rules aur common mistakes – ek hi jagah.

📄 All Required Certificates

  • 10th & 12th Marksheet
  • Graduation Certificate (if required)
  • Aadhar Card / Voter ID
  • Caste Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate
  • EWS Certificate
  • Disability Certificate (if any)
  • Ex-serviceman Certificate
  • Character Certificate
  • Passport size photos

🖼 Photo & Signature Rules

  • White/light background photo
  • No cap, goggles, filters
  • Recent photo (last 3–6 months)
  • Signature black/blue pen
  • JPG/JPEG format only
  • Usually 20kb – 200kb size
  • No blur or shadow
  • Full face visible

⚠ Common Form Mistakes

  • Wrong name spelling
  • DOB mismatch with marksheet
  • Wrong category selection
  • Invalid photo/sign upload
  • Wrong mobile/email
  • Incomplete form
  • Wrong exam center
  • Payment not confirmed

🏃 Physical & Medical Rules

  • Height & chest standards
  • Weight & BMI check
  • Running test rules
  • Long jump / high jump
  • Eye sight standards
  • Color blindness test
  • Flat foot / knock knee
  • Tattoo rules

📑 Verification Process

  • Original documents required
  • Multiple photocopies
  • Photo ID mandatory
  • Self-attested copies
  • Biometric verification
  • Category & address check
  • Education proof check
  • Final merit confirmation

❌ Rejection Reasons

  • Fake certificate
  • Wrong information
  • Blur photo/signature
  • Physical standard fail
  • Medical unfit
  • Multiple forms
  • Incomplete documents
  • Mismatch in verification
